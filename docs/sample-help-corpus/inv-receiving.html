Receiving Receipts in Oracle Inventory

Create receiving receipts against a purchase order in the Receiving work area or via the receivingReceiptRequests REST resource. Receipt routing can be standard receipt, inspection required, or direct delivery.

A receipt creates receiving transactions and accrual accounting events in Subledger Accounting (XLA_AE_LINES) that later transfer to the General Ledger via Create Accounting.

Correction and return-to-supplier transactions reverse or adjust the original receipt while preserving the audit trail of receiving transactions.
