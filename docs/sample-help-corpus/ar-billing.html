Billing and AutoInvoice in Oracle Receivables

Transaction sources feed billing lines into the AutoInvoice interface; the Import AutoInvoice scheduled process validates and creates the receivables transactions (invoices, credit memos, debit memos).

Each completed transaction generates accounting in Subledger Accounting (XLA_AE_LINES) under the Receivables event model, which transfers to GL_JE_LINES via Create Accounting.

The bill-to and ship-to customers are TCA parties; revenue recognition and tax are derived from the transaction line and the party's account profile.
